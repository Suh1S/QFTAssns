Rules of the quiz:
1. Quiz will have 4 rounds: Very Easy, Easy, Medium and Hard with 5 questions each.
2. There will be 3 "transition questions" i.e. questions b/w Very Easy and Easy, Easy and Medium, Medium and Hard for which I'll require artist name, song name and anime to award points. No partial points for these.
3. There will be another bonus round after the Hard round ends, and will have 3 questions.
4. Point distribution is: Very Easy-> 1 point, Easy-> 2 points, Medium-> 3 points, Hard-> 4 points
5. Transition questions have 2 points, regardless of difficulty
6. The bonus round questions are 3 points each.
7. First (fastest :P) 2 to answer, get points
